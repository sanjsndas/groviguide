Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Groviguide ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you visit our website or use our services. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. Please read this policy carefully to understand how your personal information is handled.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services effectively. This includes:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other identifying data you provide when scheduling an appointment or submitting feedback.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you access and interact with our website, including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and duration of visits.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Small files stored on your device to personalize your experience, track website performance, and deliver relevant content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and manage our consulting and layout planning services.
  • Respond to your inquiries, requests, and feedback effectively.
  • Improve and personalize the content and user experience on our website.
  • Send you important updates, newsletters, and promotional materials with your consent.
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activities and maintain the security of our systems.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, under strict confidentiality agreements. We may also disclose your information as required by law or to protect our rights and property.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and gather information about website usage. You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings.

We may also use analytics services that collect statistical data to improve our site but do not identify individual users.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no data transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but strive to protect your information.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access and obtain a copy of your data.
  • The right to correct or update inaccurate information.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • The right to object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • The right to withdraw consent where processing is based on your consent.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or comply with legal requirements.

8. International Transfers

Your personal data may be processed or stored in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure adequate protection of your data in these circumstances in accordance with applicable laws.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page regularly for the latest information.

11. Detailed Information About The Types Of Personal Data We Collect

Beyond the basic personal identification data, we may collect additional data depending on your interaction with us, such as:

  • Communication preferences and history.
  • Feedback, surveys, and responses to questionnaires.
  • Technical data like device type, IP address, operating system.
  • Location data if permission is granted through your device or browser.

12. Legal Bases for Processing Personal Data

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Your consent.
  • Performance of a contract or to take steps at your request before entering into a contract.
  • Compliance with legal obligations.
  • Legitimate interests pursued by our company or third parties, balanced against your rights.

13. Use of Automated Decision-Making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling processes that have legal or significant effects on you.

14. Data Protection Officer

We have appointed a Data Protection Officer responsible for overseeing data protection compliance. You may contact our DPO regarding privacy concerns.

15. Your Privacy Rights in Detail

Access to Information

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data we hold. We will provide this information within a reasonable timeframe and in an accessible format.

Correction and Update

If you find information we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request correction or updates to keep your data accurate.

Deletion and Erasure

Subject to certain exceptions, you can request us to delete your personal information if you believe we no longer need it or it was unlawfully processed.

Objection and Restriction

You may object to certain uses of your data, such as direct marketing, or request restrictions on processing while issues are resolved.

Data Portability

Where applicable, you have the right to receive a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used electronic format.

16. Cookies and Tracking Technologies in Detail

We use cookies and similar technologies to provide enhanced and personalized user experiences. These tools help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns.
  • Deliver relevant advertising when applicable.

You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings and opt-out of certain tracking by following guidelines on our site or third parties.

17. Third Party Services

We may share data with third-party providers for analytics, marketing, hosting, and customer service. These partners adhere to privacy regulations and only process data as instructed by us.

18. International and Cross-Border Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored in countries with different data protection laws. We take necessary safeguards, such as standard contractual clauses, to protect your data during such transfers.

19. Security Measures

We use industry-standard security protocols to protect information including encryption, firewalls, secure servers, and access controls.

20. Retention Periods

Personal data is kept only as long as necessary to fulfill services or comply with legal requirements. Criteria such as the nature of data and legal obligations determine retention periods.
